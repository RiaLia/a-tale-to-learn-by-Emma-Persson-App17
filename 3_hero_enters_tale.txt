And in on a crocked broomstick a silver haired, beautiful and clever witch came flying.
She wore a bow and arrows out of shiny silver on her back. Magical poison covering the ends of the arrows. 
She promised the fairy queen that she should find the heart and restore it down in the dungeons.
And forever by the witches side was a tiny baby dragon named Squirt.
But they had to hurry to retrieve the heart before Dr Jekyll could use the heart for whatever his plan was.
